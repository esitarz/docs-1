Published: Fri 16 March 2018
By OrderCloud.io

In authentication.

__Developer Access

In order to make it easier for developers to get in and start testing applications we made a unique role called DevCenterImpersonate. This role allows you to log into your application with your Dev Center username and password so you don't have to go through the trouble of creating a user first.

To take advantage of this feature simply include the role DevCenterImpersonate in the scope of your authentication request:
